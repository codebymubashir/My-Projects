Our Promise

Nothing rushed. Nothing frozen. Nothing forgotten on the stove.

Every karahi at Mezbaan is cooked to a single order, over an open flame, the way our founder's mother cooked for guests who dropped by unannounced — because in this house, a guest was never an inconvenience.

That idea is still the whole menu. Slow rice, hand-kneaded bread, and a kitchen that never asks you to wait for the wrong reasons.
