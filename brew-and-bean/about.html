Our Journey

From one roaster to a neighborhood favorite

Brew & Bean began in 2016 with a single second-hand roaster and a passion for great coffee. What started as a small stall has grown into a warm, welcoming café loved by locals and travelers alike.

We still believe in doing things the slow way — sourcing responsibly, roasting in small batches, and treating every customer like family.

  • ✓ Ethically sourced, single-origin beans
  • ✓ Family-owned and independently run
  • ✓ A space built for community
