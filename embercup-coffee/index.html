Why altitude

Three reasons we don't buy low.

Elevation is the single biggest lever on flavor complexity — more than processing method, more than roast level.

01

Slower ripening

Cooler air at elevation slows cherry development, giving sugars more time to build inside the bean.

02

Denser structure

Dense beans roast more evenly and hold acidity longer — the backbone of a bright, structured cup.

03

Traceable lots

Every bag lists the farm, elevation, and harvest window — not just a country of origin.
