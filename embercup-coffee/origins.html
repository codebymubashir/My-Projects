Processing methods

How the cherry becomes the bean.

Processing shapes flavor as much as origin does — here's the three methods behind our current lots.

WASHED

Clean & bright

Fruit pulp removed before drying — the clearest expression of the bean itself, high acidity.

HONEY

Balanced sweetness

Some mucilage left on during drying, adding body and syrupy sweetness without overpowering.

NATURAL

Fruit-forward

Whole cherry dried intact — heavy fruit, wine-like fermentation notes, lower acidity.
